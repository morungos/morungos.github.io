Stuart Watt is a cognitive scientist and professional software developer with years of experience researching and developing intelligent systems for complex, data-rich applications. He has worked in fields ranging from business and psychology, art and philosophy, to technology and healthcare, and has more than eighty peer-reviewed publications across these fields.

He is currently co-founder and chief technology officer for Turalt, bringing together AI and cognitive models to give people real-time feedback, advice, and analytics on their email communication.

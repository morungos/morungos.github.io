--- layout: page title: About me: Stuart Watt ---

I am a cognitive scientist and professional software developer with years of experience researching and developing intelligent systems for complex, data-rich applications. I have worked in fields ranging from art and philosophy, business and psychology, to technology and healthcare, and have more than sixty peer-reviewed publications across these fields. I currently work for the University Health Network, where my experience in the practice of modern development techniques and the successful design and deployment of usable systems is helping build a range of mobile and web-based decision-support systems to assist in healthcare.
